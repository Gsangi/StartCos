Case Study :

The Company

An Auto Ancillary with supply to a world Leader OEM.

The Problem

Caught between demand and availability of Transport equipment inventory, the Company struggles with legacy issues of high costs and underutilization while lagging industry counterparts on key measures such as profitability, shipping, etc. Company seeks to improve the Shipping process with available Resources and a change process focused on cost-effectiveness and productivity improvement in of the business.

The Approach

Every dayr, the Company assembles and ships over 42 types of Products and each quantity varying from 1500 to 2200 numbers. We focused on some aspect of a critical productivity issue: inventory turns, product build/rebuild cycle times, design of Shipping equipment, etc. During intensive three-day problem-solving sessions, designed and facilitated by us, we analysed and developed recommendations for addressing these issues. At session close, we presented recommendations to the Company’s Executive Committee who must evaluated and rendered decisions on the spot. Implementation began immediately and progress was checked weekly. Project completion and evaluation took place after 12 weeks.

The Results

In six months’, time it was observed that our changed design and Process had achieved an year to year benefit of 10 Million Rs.
